# 🧠 Biological Neuron - Quick Revision

## Definition

A Biological Neuron is the basic structural and functional unit of the nervous system that receives, processes, and transmits information using electrical and chemical signals.

---

# Main Parts

## 1. Dendrites

✔ Receive signals from other neurons.

---

## 2. Cell Body (Soma)

✔ Processes the received signals.
✔ Contains the nucleus.

---

## 3. Axon

✔ Carries electrical impulses away from the cell body.

---

## 4. Synapse

✔ Small gap between two neurons.
✔ Transfers signals using neurotransmitters.

---

# Signal Flow

Dendrites

Cell Body

# Memory Trick

**DCAS**

# Important Points

✅ Biological neuron is the basic unit of the nervous system.

✅ Human brain contains approximately 86 billion neurons.

✅ Neurons communicate using electrical and chemical signals.

✅ Artificial neurons are inspired by biological neurons.

# One-Line Summary

A Biological Neuron receives information through dendrites, processes it in the cell body, sends it through the axon, and communicates with the next neuron through the synapse.
