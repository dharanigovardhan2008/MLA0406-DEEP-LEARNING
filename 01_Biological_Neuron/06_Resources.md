# 📚 Learning Resources - Biological Neuron

## 📖 Recommended Books

### 1. Deep Learning
**Authors:** Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, Aaron Courville

Why Read?
- Standard reference book for Deep Learning.
- Explains Artificial Neural Networks and their biological inspiration.

---

### 2. Neural Networks and Deep Learning
**Author:** Michael Nielsen

Why Read?
- Beginner-friendly.
- Free online book with clear explanations.

Website:
https://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/

---

### 3. Deep Learning with Python
**Author:** François Chollet

Why Read?
- Practical implementation using Python and Keras.
- Good for beginners moving from theory to coding.
